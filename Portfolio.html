Photography


For this photoshoot , I had my subject surrounded by nature to portray strength and elegance , showcase their afro to fully define their beauty and wear earthy tones to fit the aesthetic of the location.

Following the compositional guidelines the couple becomes the center of attention I played a lot with the shutter spped , aperture and exposure to make sure the it compliments each of their skin tones. I was able to acheive this photoshoot with a Canon T7I camera, tripod and Gold reflector, and an ETTL flash.

These were my camera settings;
Iso-3200
Aperture-5.6
Shutter speed- 1/50th
Focal length-35mm
